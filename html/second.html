Karasuno High


Karasuno High’s volleyball team rebuilds under Coach Ukai and faculty advisor Takeda, pushing past their reputation as “The Flightless Crows.” With Hinata’s explosive speed, Kageyama’s pinpoint sets, and Nishinoya’s fearless defense, the team evolves fast. Core players like Daichi, Sugawara, and Asahi bring stability, while Tsukishima and Yamaguchi sharpen Karasuno’s edge. Kiyoko Shimizu, the composed team manager, is joined by Yachi Hitoka, the bright and eager newcomer who quickly finds her place. Every match is a step toward national-level play.
