Haikyu!! follows Shōyō Hinata, a high school student who dreams of becoming a great volleyball player despite his short stature. Inspired by a legendary player known as "The Little Giant," he joins Karasuno High School’s volleyball team and develops a fierce rivalry and eventual partnership with talented setter Tobio Kageyama.

All About The Author:

Haruichi Furudate is a Japanese manga artist best known for creating Haikyū!!. Born in Iwate Prefecture, Furudate began their career with one-shots and short stories before debuting Haikyū!! in Weekly Shōnen Jump in 2012. The series quickly gained popularity for its dynamic sports action, emotional storytelling, and detailed character development. Furudate is known for being private, with little public information about their personal life, but their passion for volleyball and storytelling shines through in their work.
