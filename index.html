Haikyu!! follows Shōyō Hinata, a high school student who dreams of becoming a great volleyball player despite his short stature. Inspired by a legendary player known as "The Little Giant," he joins Karasuno High School’s volleyball team and develops a fierce rivalry and eventual partnership with talented setter Tobio Kageyama. Together, they push each other to grow, transforming Karasuno from an underdog team into a formidable force on the court.

The series explores the intense world of competitive high school volleyball, where every match is a test of strategy, endurance, and teamwork. As Hinata and Kageyama evolve as players, so does the entire Karasuno squad. Each teammate brings a unique personality and skillset, contributing to the team's growth both on and off the court. Haikyu!! introduces a wide range of rival schools such as Aoba Johsai, Nekoma, Shiratorizawa, and Inarizaki, each with their own philosophies and playstyles that challenge Karasuno in different ways. Through rigorous training, emotional setbacks, and unforgettable tournaments, the story highlights the value of perseverance, friendship, and the pursuit of excellence. Whether you're drawn to the fast-paced action, the heartfelt character development, or the inspiring message of rising above limitations, Haikyu!! delivers a powerful and uplifting experience that resonates far beyond the volleyball court.

All About The Author:

Haruichi Furudate is a Japanese manga artist best known for creating Haikyū!!. Born in Iwate Prefecture, Furudate began their career with one-shots and short stories before debuting Haikyū!! in Weekly Shōnen Jump in 2012. The series quickly gained popularity for its dynamic sports action, emotional storytelling, and detailed character development. Furudate is known for being private, with little public information about their personal life, but their passion for volleyball and storytelling shines through in their work.
