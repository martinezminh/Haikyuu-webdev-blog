Nekoma High


Nekoma High’s volleyball team thrives under Coach Nekomata and assistant coach Kai, known for their calm strategy and data-driven play. With Kuroo’s tactical blocks, Kenma’s cerebral setting, and Yaku’s sharp defense, the team controls the court with precision. Key players like Lev, Yamamoto, and Inuoka bring energy and unpredictability, while Shibayama anchors the libero role. Team manager Nozomi Tanaka supports the squad behind the scenes. Every match is a calculated step toward dominating at the national level.
