Season 1

The first season of Haikyu!! follows Shoyo Hinata, a determined middle schooler inspired by a small player’s performance at nationals. After failing to form a team, he joins Karasuno High and clashes with star setter Kageyama. Covering manga Chapters 1–71, the season explores their rivalry, evolving teamwork, and Karasuno’s push to become a top-tier squad.

Season 1

Season 2 of Haikyu!! follows Karasuno’s intense training and evolution as they prepare for the Spring High tournament. With new strategies, fresh rivalries, and the addition of middle blocker Kei Tsukishima’s growth arc, the team sharpens its skills through grueling practice matches—including a pivotal training camp with powerhouse schools like Nekoma, Fukurodani, and Ubugawa. Covering manga Chapters 72 to 149 (Volumes 9 to 16), this season dives deep into teamwork, character development, and Karasuno’s climb toward national-level competition.

Season 3

Season 3 of Haikyu!! focuses entirely on Karasuno vs. Shiratorizawa in the Miyagi Prefecture finals. Covering manga Chapters 150 to 190, it’s a high-stakes, single-match season where power clashes with strategy, and every point pushes Karasuno closer to nationals.

Season 4

Season 4 of Haikyu!! is split into two parts and covers Karasuno’s journey to the national tournament. The first half focuses on training, new techniques, and the fierce Tokyo qualifiers, while the second half dives into the Nationals arc, starting with Karasuno vs. Tsubakihara and the intense match against Inarizaki High. It adapts manga Chapters 191 to 292, spanning Volumes 22 to 33.
