Aoba Johsai High


Aoba Johsai High (Seijoh) is one of Miyagi Prefecture’s top four volleyball teams. They’ve consistently reached the finals of major tournaments like Interhigh and Spring High, often falling just short against Shiratorizawa. Most of their players came from Kitagawa Daiichi Junior High and were invited to join Seijoh’s competitive squad. Their strength centers around Tōru Oikawa, the team captain and setter, known for his powerful jump serve and ability to bring out the best in his teammates. Alongside him are Iwaizumi, the vice-captain and ace, and Kyōtani, a second-year with explosive spikes but a rough attitude. Seijoh is famous for its teamwork, tactical plays, and bold serves, earning respect across the region.
